# Continuing Education & Training Grants

## Key Programs & Grants

### Iowa Workforce Development Grants
**Training grants for high-demand occupations**
- **Programs:**
  - Last-Dollar Scholarship (for high-demand occupations)
  - Registered Apprenticeship Grants
  - Summer Youth Internship Program
- **Website:** [workforce.iowa.gov/opportunities/grants](https://workforce.iowa.gov/opportunities/grants)
- **All Recent Programs:** [workforce.iowa.gov/list](https://workforce.iowa.gov/list)
- **Job Training Grants:** [workforce.iowa.gov/job-training-grants](https://workforce.iowa.gov/opportunities/grants/job-training-grants)
- **Available Through:** Local community colleges and workforce centers

### Workforce Training and Economic Development Fund (WTED)
**Program innovation and career/technical education**
- **Administered by:** Iowa Department of Education
- **Supports:** Community college industry-focused training and retraining programs
- **Contact:**
  - Robin Shaffer Lilienthal, Administrative Consultant, Community Colleges
  - Email: robin.lilienthal@iowa.gov
  - Phone: (515) 418-8273
- **Website:** [educate.iowa.gov/wted](https://educate.iowa.gov/higher-ed/community-colleges/iowa-skilled-worker-job-creation-fund/wted)

### Iowa Jobs Training Program (260F)
**Upskilling for existing businesses and their workforce**
- **Purpose:** Helps existing Iowa businesses upskill their current workforce
- **Contact:**
  - Lauren Siebert, IWD 260 Program Manager
  - Email: lauren.siebert@iwd.iowa.gov
- **Website:** [workforce.iowa.gov/260f](https://workforce.iowa.gov/employers/build/training/260f)
- **How to Apply:** Contact local community college for application assistance

### Iowa New Jobs Training Program (260E)
**Training for businesses creating new jobs**
- **Purpose:** Supports businesses creating new jobs in Iowa by funding employee training
- **How to Apply:** Contact local community college for application assistance

## How to Apply or Learn More

### For Individuals (Iowa Residents)
1. **Contact Kirkwood Community College's Workforce Development office** at (319) 398-1022
2. Discuss your career goals and training needs
3. Get guidance on grants and tuition assistance you might qualify for
4. Apply for programs with their assistance

### For Businesses
1. **Contact Kirkwood's business training team** at (319) 398-1022
2. Discuss your workforce training needs
3. Access business-focused grants like 260F and 260E
4. Develop customized training programs for employees

### For Statewide Questions
- **Iowa Workforce Development** is your central resource
- **Website:** [workforce.iowa.gov](https://workforce.iowa.gov/)
- Can point you to staff overseeing workforce grants
