## ðŸš« What Peer Support Is NOT

To protect both peers and participants, it's important to be clear about boundaries:

**Peer support does NOT provide:**
- Clinical therapy or counseling (requires licensed professional)
- Diagnosis or medical advice (see a doctor)
- Legal services (consult an attorney)
- Emergency or crisis response (call 911 or 988)
- Case management (offered by agencies)
- Housing placement (work with housing agencies)
- Financial assistance (see community resources)

**If you need any of the above services, please see the [Community Resources Directory](resources/) for appropriate contacts.**
