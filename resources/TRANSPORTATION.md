## Tips for Using Public Transit

### Getting Started
1. **Find your route:** Use Transit app or Google Maps
2. **Check schedules:** Times vary by route and day
3. **Plan extra time:** Especially when learning a new route
4. **Carry exact change or use app:** Not all buses make change
5. **Ask the driver:** Bus drivers can help with route questions

### Transfers
- **Iowa City ↔ Coralville:** Free transfers between systems
- **380 Express:** Separate fare required
- **CAMBUS:** Free connections

### Accessibility
- All fixed-route buses are ADA accessible
- Wheelchair lifts available
- Priority seating for seniors and people with disabilities
- Paratransit (SEATS) available for those who qualify

### Safety
- Wait at designated bus stops
- Stay behind the yellow line when boarding
- Hold handrails when bus is moving
- Notify driver if you need assistance

### Learning to Trust the Bus
**Recovery sticks when it has texture. Learning to trust the bus—one ride at a time.**

- Start with a short, familiar route during non-peak hours
- Practice with a friend if possible
- Give yourself extra time—there's no rush
- Use the Transit app to track your bus in real time
- Each ride builds confidence
