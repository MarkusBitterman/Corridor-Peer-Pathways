## ðŸ”‘ Core Principles

### Peer Support, Explained Plainly
- Grounded in **lived experience**
- Built on **trust, dignity, and mutual respect**
- Clear about what peer support does (and doesn't do)

### Scope of Role (Trust is Infrastructure)
**What peer support does:**
- Goal-setting and accountability
- Skill-building and confidence-building
- Resource navigation (local, real, current)
- Community reintegration
- Walking alongsideâ€”not directing from above

**What it does not do:**
- Clinical therapy
- Diagnosis or medical advice
- Legal services
- Emergency or crisis response

This clarity protects peers *and* participants.

### Local Resources (Johnson & Linn Counties)
No shame. No jargon. No maze without a legend.

### Recovery as Practice (Not Just Talk)
Recovery sticks when it has texture:
- Mindfulness, medication consistency, journaling
- Movement and walking the Corridor cities
- Learning to trust the busâ€”one ride at a time
- Building confidence through doing
