## Important Tips

- **Hours and services may change; call ahead whenever possible**
- Many sites also assist with diapers, hygiene items, and nutrition services
- If you need help navigating resources or online forms, local libraries and 211 operators offer individual support
- Most food pantries serve residents of specific areas—check eligibility when calling
