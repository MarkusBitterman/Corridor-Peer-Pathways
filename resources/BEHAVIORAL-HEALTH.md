# Behavioral Health & Mental Health Resources

## Linn County

### Linn County Mental Health Access Center
**24/7 walk-in crisis services for adults 18+**
- **Address:** 501 13th St. NW, Cedar Rapids, IA
- **Services:** Mental health crisis, substance use disorder crisis, suicide risk assessment, screening, stabilization, outpatient services, referrals
- **Available:** 24/7 walk-in (no appointment needed)
- **Website:** [linncountyiowa.gov/Mental-Health-Access-Center](https://www.linncountyiowa.gov/1423/Mental-Health-Access-Center)
- **Partners:** Abbe Health, Area Ambulance Service, Foundation 2 Crisis Services

### Abbe Center for Community Mental Health
**Outpatient and community mental health services**
- **Address:** 520 11th St. NW, Cedar Rapids, IA 52405
- **Phone:** (319) 398-3562
- **Services:** Outpatient mental health services for all ages

### Saint Luke's Counseling Center
**Therapy and counseling services**
- **Address:** 225 Coe Rd NE, Cedar Rapids, IA 52402
- **Phone:** (319) 369-7952
- **Services:** DBT, CBT, group/family therapy

### Tanager Place
**Children and adolescent mental health services**
- **Address:** 2309 C Street SW, Cedar Rapids, IA 52404
- **Phone:** (319) 365-9164
- **Services:** Telehealth, outpatient, residential care for children and adolescents

### Linn County Mental Health Resources

## Important Information

- **Crisis lines are available 24/7 for immediate support**
- **Walk-in crisis services are available at Linn County Mental Health Access Center**
- Most clinics accept insurance; some offer sliding scale fees
- Many services available via telehealth
- For immediate crisis, call 988 or visit a walk-in crisis center
