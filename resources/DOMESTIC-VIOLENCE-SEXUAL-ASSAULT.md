# Domestic Violence & Sexual Assault Support

## Johnson County

### Domestic Violence Intervention Program (DVIP) & Rape Victim Advocacy Program (RVAP)
**24/7 crisis support, shelter, advocacy, and counseling**
- **24/7 Crisis Line:** 800-373-1043
- **Local Domestic Violence Crisis Line:** (319) 351-1043
- **Rape Crisis Hotline:** 800-228-1625
- **Office Address:** 1105 S. Gilbert Ct., Ste. 300, Iowa City, IA 52240
- **Website:** [dvipiowa.org](https://dvipiowa.org/)
- **Services:**
  - Emergency shelter
  - Advocacy and counseling
  - Support groups
  - Housing advocacy
  - Legal and medical advocacy
  - Youth and family support
  - Community education
- **All services are free and confidential**

### Amani Community Services
**Culturally specific support for African American community**
- **Crisis Line:** (319) 804-0741
- **Website:** [amani-cs.org](https://www.amani-cs.org/)
- **Services:** Culturally responsive domestic violence support and advocacy

## Important Information

- **If you are in immediate danger, call 911**
- All services are **free and confidential**
- Available to survivors **regardless of age, gender, identity, or circumstance**
- Advocates can assist with:
  - Safety planning
  - Emergency shelter
  - Legal and medical processes
  - Ongoing emotional support
