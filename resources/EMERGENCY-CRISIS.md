## 24/7 Crisis Support

### 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
**National crisis line for mental health, suicide, or substance use emergencies**
- **Phone:** 988 (call or text)
- **Available:** 24/7
- **Services:** Crisis counseling, suicide prevention, emotional support

### Your Life Iowa
**Free, confidential 24/7 crisis and mental health support for all Iowans**
- **Phone:** 1-855-581-8111
- **Text:** 1-855-895-8398
- **Website:** [yourlifeiowa.org](https://yourlifeiowa.org/)
- **Available:** 24/7
- **Services:** Crisis intervention, counseling, alcohol/drug/gambling support, mental health resources, referrals
- **Operated by:** Foundation 2 in partnership with Iowa Department of Health and Human Services

### Foundation 2 Crisis Line (Linn County)
**24/7 crisis intervention services**
- **Phone:** (319) 362-2174
- **Available:** 24/7

## Crisis Walk-In Services

### Linn County Mental Health Access Center
**24/7 walk-in crisis services for adults 18+**
- **Address:** 501 13th St. NW, Cedar Rapids, IA
- **Services:** Mental health crisis support, substance use disorder crisis, suicide risk assessment, screening, stabilization, outpatient services, referrals
- **Available:** 24/7 walk-in (no appointment needed)
- **Website:** [linncountyiowa.gov/Mental-Health-Access-Center](https://www.linncountyiowa.gov/1423/Mental-Health-Access-Center)
- **Partners:** Abbe Health, Area Ambulance Service, Foundation 2 Crisis Services

## Statewide Resources

### Iowa Victim Service Call Center
**24/7 support for crime victims**
- **Phone:** 800-770-1650
- **Text:** Text "iowahelp" to 20121
- **Available:** 24/7
- **Services:** Crime victim advocacy, safety planning, resource navigation

### 2-1-1 Iowa
**Information and referral service for community resources**
- **Phone:** 211 (from landline) or 319-739-4211 or 1-866-469-2211
- **Mobile App:** Search "211 Iowa" in app stores
- **Website:** [211iowa.org](http://www.211iowa.org/)
- **Available:** 24/7
- **Services:** Referrals to local services, food pantries, shelters, and emergency assistance
