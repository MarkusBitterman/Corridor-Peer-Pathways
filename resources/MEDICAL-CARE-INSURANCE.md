## Important Information

### Access to Care
- **All free clinics serve people regardless of insurance status or ability to pay**
- **Most clinics offer sliding scale fees based on income**
- **No one should be turned away due to inability to pay**

### What to Bring
- Photo ID
- Proof of residency (utility bill, lease)
- Proof of income (pay stubs, benefit letters)
- Current medication list
- Insurance cards (if applicable)

### Getting Help
- **Call ahead:** Confirm hours and services
- **Ask about enrollment:** Many clinics help with insurance enrollment
- **Be honest:** Staff are there to help, not judge
- **Follow up:** Schedule follow-up appointments as needed

### Emergency Care
- **If you have a medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room**
- **Emergency rooms cannot turn you away due to inability to pay**
- **Financial assistance programs are available at hospitals—ask social work staff**
