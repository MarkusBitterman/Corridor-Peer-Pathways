## üìù How to Use This Directory

### For Peers and Participants
1. **Browse by category** to find the type of support you need
2. **Call ahead** when possible to confirm hours and services
3. **Bring required documents** (ID, proof of residency, income information)
4. **Ask for help navigating** - staff at most organizations are trained to help
5. **No shame, no judgment** - these services exist to help you

### For Peer Support Specialists
- Use this directory to help participants navigate local resources
- Keep printed copies for reference during sessions
- Share relevant sections as needed
- Update participants on any changes you notice
- Remember: **Peer support is not crisis intervention** - refer to appropriate services

### For Community Members
- **Fork it** - This information is public domain
- **Localize it** - Adapt for your community
- **Print it** - Make physical copies for distribution
- **Update it** - Submit corrections or additions
- **Teach from it** - Use in training or education
